The pipeline

One upload. Five steps. Hands off.

Your own material is the source of truth — your notes, your words — so every output sounds like you, not a template.

Submit

Upload what you have — a slide deck, a document, a PDF, or paste your notes — and pick the knowledge domain it belongs to.

Script

Your notes and text become natural spoken narration and flowing prose. Nothing written down? It drafts from your material.

Voice

Studio-grade text-to-speech, or a clone of your own voice on higher tiers.

Render

Slides + audio composited to 1080p/4K video with synced captions; the article, notes, quiz and more generated alongside.

Publish

Push to LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube and your feed on a schedule — Substack handled manually. You approve before anything goes live.
